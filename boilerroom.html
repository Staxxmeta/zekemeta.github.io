BOILER CLASSIFICATIONS
LOW PRESSURE
Steam boilers operating at or below 15 PSI. Hot water boilers below 160 PSI / 250°F.
HIGH PRESSURE
Steam boilers operating above 15 PSI. Hot water boilers above 160 PSI or 250°F.
POWER BOILER
High pressure steam boiler used to generate power. Governed by ASME Section I.
HEATING BOILER
Low pressure boiler used for space heating. Governed by ASME Section IV.
SAFETY VALVE REQUIREMENTS
SET PRESSURE
Must not exceed the maximum allowable working pressure (MAWP) of the boiler.
CAPACITY
Must be able to discharge all steam the boiler can generate without pressure rising more than 6% above the highest set pressure.
TESTING
Must be manually tested at least once per year by lifting the test lever when boiler is at operating pressure.
